Latitude and the Temperature


Max Temperature by Latitude shows a very strong relationship between the maximum observed temperature for a city and its latitude, however the fulcrum of this observation is not at the equator, but at about 20 degrees N. latitude, with max temps dropping off on either side of that. This would make sense as the Northern Hemisphere is currently experiencing its summer. One weakness in this sample data is that it appears to include a larger number of cities in the Northern Hemisphere than in the Southern, but nevertheless, this trend is very strong.
